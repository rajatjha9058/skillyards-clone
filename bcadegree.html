Frequently Asked Questions

How is the Skillyards On-The Job BCA Degree Program different from traditional BCA degrees? ▼

Skillyards On-The Job BCA Degree Program is different in many ways than the traditional BCA, such as practical training on client projects, unlike theory-based classroom training. Additionally, students work as Software Developer Interns with a monthly stipend of up to 15000. Upon completing their internship, they get a 100% guaranteed in-house placement with a package of up to 10 LPA as a Software Developer before graduation.

Are there any scholarship opportunities available? ▼

Students get a scholarship of up to 70% based on their practical knowledge to help them easily pay the fees.

Is EMI available in the On-The Job BCA Degree Program? ▼

Yes, the On-The-Job BCA Program offers an EMI option so that students won’t feel pressured to pay the program fees all at once.
